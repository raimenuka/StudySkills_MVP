Study Skills-Exams

Multiple choice

  1. This means that the answer to the question must be chosen from a range of possible answers, given to you as part of the question. You will be asked to choose the answer that you think best fits the question. Be sure to read your instructions to candidates section carefully.
  2. For example: Question - Which is the capital of Sweden?
    • a. Gdansk

    • b. Stockholm

    • c. Helsinki

    • d. Helsingborg

    • e. Volvo

  3. You would usually be required to indicate the correct answer by writing/circle/tick your answer as (a), (b), (c), (d), or (e) in your examination book.
  4. In a web-based examination format you would be asked to click on the appropriate answer. Or on the button next to the correct answer.
