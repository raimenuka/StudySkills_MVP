Study Skills-Exams

Open Book

  1. An open book exam means that you can take your notes, specified books and other references into the exam room. This will probably vary with the subject and the lecturer concerned.

  2. Open book exams can be a trap because you might think that you do not need to concentrate on revising the subject to the same extent as a closed book exam. WRONG!

  3. There are three main areas that your preparation must encompass:
    • Prepare properly
    • Know your subject
    • Making revision notes
