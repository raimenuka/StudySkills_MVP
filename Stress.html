Study Skills-Exams

Controlling stress and anxiety

It is normal to experience some level of anxiety or stress just before and during an examination. This is appropriate and helps spur us on. However, occasionally our stress levels might surge unexpectedly and throw us off balance at just the wrong moment.

Dealing with exam anxiety

  • Stay healthy and physically strong - practise relaxation.
  • Start revision early and stick to your revision plan.
  • Acknowledge your competence.

    On the way/outside the exam

  • You will never know everything.
  • No perfect answers.
  • Exam not designed to fail you
  • Most people end up passing
  • Avoid discussing possible exam questions
  • Try to stay relaxed by focusing on calming
  • Focus your thoughts

    • MORE INFO

    Waiting/During the exam

  • Refocus: Try to refocus your energy.
  • Reassess: Decide whether to persevere with the question.
  • Remember: You do not have to give perfect answers

    Fear of the unknown

  • Physical symptoms:heart starts to race, breathing becomes shallower, edgier than usual
  • Fear of the unknown:Stay healthy, physically strong ... practise relaxation .
